Behind The Mask Scrub

Glamour is a beauty brand born from passion, resilience, and creativity. It was created by Katlo Kesigo, a broke teenager in Botswana who dreamed of making cosmetics affordable and accessible for people of all ages. What began as a small vision grew into a brand focused on confidence, self-expression, and inclusivity. Glamour believes beauty should never feel out of reach, offering stylish, quality products at budget-friendly prices. With a youthful spirit and inspiring beginnings, the brand represents determination, empowerment, and the idea that anyone can feel beautiful without spending a fortune
